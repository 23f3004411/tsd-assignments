Key Metric: Net Profit Margin

This formula shows the percentage of revenue left after all expenses have been deducted.

$$ \text{Net Profit Margin} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Revenue}} \times 100\% $$

Our Q3 Margin: $ \frac{\$264M}{\$1.2B} \times 100\% = 22\% $
